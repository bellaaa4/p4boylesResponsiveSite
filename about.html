About Us

101 Bagels & Subs: A Coastal Classic in the Heart of Oceanside

101 Bagels & Subs in Oceanside, California, has become a beloved neighborhood spot known for its freshly baked bagels, hearty sandwiches, and friendly, down-to-earth atmosphere. Located at 323 North Coast Highway, the shop takes pride in crafting its bagels daily from traditional recipes, achieving the perfect balance of a crisp outer crust and soft, chewy interior. Over the years, 101 Bagels has built a loyal following among locals, commuters, and visitors alike, many of whom stop in for a quick breakfast sandwich, a lunch break sub, or a cup of coffee paired with a warm, fresh-from-the-oven bagel. Beyond serving walk-in customers, the business also caters to the Oceanside community by offering take-out, dine-in, and catering options for events and local offices, making it a versatile choice for both everyday meals and special occasions. Known for its friendly service and welcoming vibe, 101 Bagels & Subs reflects the laid-back coastal charm of Oceanside while maintaining the quality and consistency of a classic bagel shop, earning it a solid reputation as a local favorite and a staple in the downtown area’s growing food scene.

What Makes 101 Bagels & Subs Truly One of a Kind

What sets 101 Bagels & Subs apart is its perfect blend of authenticity, community spirit, and coastal charm. Unlike larger chains, this Oceanside favorite bakes its bagels fresh every morning using traditional methods that deliver a chewy, flavorful bite you can taste in every sandwich. Each order is made to perfection, with locally sourced ingredients and a genuine passion for quality. But it’s not just about the food—101 Bagels stands out for its warm, welcoming atmosphere where customers are treated like friends, not transactions. The team takes the time to remember regulars’ names and favorite orders, creating a personal touch that keeps people coming back. Their commitment to community runs deep, from catering local events to supporting nearby businesses, all while providing a cozy spot that captures the relaxed, friendly vibe of Oceanside’s coastal lifestyle.

Our Mission: Fresh Flavor, Community Connection, and Coastal Comfort

At 101 Bagels & Subs, our mission is to bring the Oceanside community together through the simple joy of fresh, handcrafted food. We are dedicated to serving high-quality bagels, sandwiches, and coffee made daily with care, using time-honored recipes and fresh ingredients. With a focus on friendly service, consistency, and community connection, we strive to create a welcoming space where every customer feels like part of the family—one bagel at a time.
